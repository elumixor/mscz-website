Frequently Asked Questions

{{1}}. What's included in the price? Isn't 6000 CZK too expensive?

The fee covers accommodation, 3 meals per day, expert guidance, and transformative experiences aimed at nurturing your spiritual growth over the course of 4 days. The prices is significantly lower than comparable festivals or similar programs and is somewhat comparable to the regular price of a hotel stay.

As a non-profit organization we aim at making spiritual practices accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation. If you are experiencing financial hardship, please each out to us to discuss potential discounts or payment plans.

{{2}}. What is your refund policy?

During online registration, a prepayment of 2000 CZK (80 EUR) is sufficient to guarantee your place at the retreat.

The remaining amount must be paid before the beginning of the retreat. You can pay in cash or by bank transfer.

When canceled before May 1st, 100% of the deposit is refunded.

After May 1st, the deposit is non-refundable.

{{3}}. I am new to these meditation practices? Will I be able to keep up?

Our programs are designed to accommodate individuals of all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. Our experienced instructors will guide you through each practice with clarity and support, ensuring a fulfilling and enriching experience for everyone.

{{4}}. What should I take with me?

What to bring:
  • Yoga mat
  • Meditation cushion (if you need one)
  • Personal hygiene items, towel
  • Comfortable clothes for asanas
  • Warm clothes (it can get chilly at night)
  • Blanket/scarf to cover yourself during shavasana
  • Slippers for indoor use
  • ID card
  • Musical instruments (are welcomed)
What not to bring:
  • Alcohol, cigarettes, drugs
  • Meat, fish, eggs, onion, and garlic
  • Perfumes, cosmetics
Clothing:

Monks are present at the retreat, so you should not wear transparent, revealing, too tight clothing that attracts attention (like short shorts, short skirts, tight leggings, tank tops, etc.).

Your clothing should be simple, modest, and comfortable.

Please do not wear perfume these days, as it is distracting at collective practices, and also odors mix when participants practice together indoors.

{{5}}. Can I have a special (vegan/lactose-free) diet?

Yes!

We offer a variety of dietary options, including vegan, vegetarian, lactose-free, and gluten-free meals.

Please inform us of any special dietary requirements when registering for the retreat, and we will do our utmost to accommodate your needs.

{{6}}. I am an atheist and don't believe in spiritual stuff. Why would I go to a retreat?

Many participants, regardless of their belief system, find value in the practices of meditation, yoga, and mindfulness for their potential to reduce stress, increase mental clarity, and enhance overall well-being. Whether you're seeking inner peace, stress relief, or simply a break from the hustle and bustle of daily life, our retreats offer a supportive environment for exploration and self-discovery.
