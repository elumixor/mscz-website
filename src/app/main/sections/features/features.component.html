Rest. Recharge. Truly. In absolute seclusion - surrounded by the protected natural landscape of Žďárské vrchy - our campsite is surrounded by a picturesque forest, creating a perfect, peaceful atmosphere.

A beautiful lake just a few steps away invites for creative walks during the breaks in the program.

The shower rooms underwent a renovation, providing guests with comfort and convenience during their stay. The fastest way to get to the retreat site is by car.

You can get there by public transportation from Prague as follows:
  1. Take the train Praha - Pardubice hl.n.
  2. Change to the train Pardubice hl.n. - Hlinsko, nadr.
  3. Take a bus to the nearest village Hlinsko, nadr - Svratouch, střed.
  4. We will then meet you and bring you to the retreat location.


Near the beginning of the retreat, we will organize the possibility of getting to the site together with other participants who are traveling by car.
